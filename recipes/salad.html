Salad Recipe

This salad recipe is the best, once you try it you will not want to make any other salad

Ingridients:

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Chop all vegetables and place them into a bowl
  2. In a seperate bowl, mix lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper
  3. Pour mixture on the the chopped vegetables

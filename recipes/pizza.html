Pizza Recipe

This is a quick pizza recipe you can make in 15 minutes

Ingridients:

  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • 00 Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Add yeast and sugar to warm bowl and mix
  3. Add flour, oil and salt. Beat until smooth
  4. Rest for a few minutes
  5. Roll dough into pizza shape
  6. Spread sauce and topping of your choosing
  7. Bake until ready
